There will be a lot of matrix multiplication, so it's important that we understand what it is and does.

For me it was never clear in my head how matrix multiplication works or why it's defined the way it is. I sort of just memorized how it works with a physical mnemonic where I turn my head to the right and then a downwards nod and that's it. A good way to think about matrix multiplication is described here. Matrix multiplication is defined the way it is because it is the composition of two linear functions, which results in a new linear function.
