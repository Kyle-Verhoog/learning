what is this?

I have a problem. The problem is that I can't find motivation to learn subjects that I find interesting, particularly math subjects.

In highschool I began to love learning. I don't know if it was the pace, the material or the atmosphere, but it worked. Once I got to university I lost the passion. I can try to pin it on people, teachers, courses, but ultimately I think the blame rests on my own shoulders. So something has to change.

I love to teach and so I'm going to try to use this project as a means to learn, teach, share and most importantly motivate.
